About Tuban

Tuban, a coastal regency in East Java, is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and natural beauty. From serene beaches and majestic caves to historical landmarks like the tomb of Sunan Bonang, Tuban offers a unique blend of relaxation and exploration. Discover the charm of this vibrant region through its traditions, landscapes, and welcoming communities.

Nglirip waterfall

Nglirip Waterfall in Tuban is a stunning hidden gem surrounded by lush greenery and dramatic cliffs. The turquoise pool at the base and the refreshing mist create a serene atmosphere, perfect for relaxing or capturing beautiful photos. The peaceful vibe, warm locals, and nearby traditional snacks make the visit even more enjoyable.
